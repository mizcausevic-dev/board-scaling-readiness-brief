# Kinetic Gain Embedded

Board Scaling Readiness Brief is designed to open an embedded executive-intelligence engagement:

- convert scattered scale signals, drag pockets, and owner-readiness evidence into one board-readable readiness packet
- tighten the leadership story around what can scale next instead of overclaiming theoretical gains
- create a reusable operating layer for committee reviews, diligence rooms, and investor updates
- prove scaling readiness without weakening the systems that still have to carry the company
